To create effective lessons, we need to understand the problems students are facing. By working directly with students in underserved populations, we can improve Oppia to create a learning environment that addresses their unique circumstances.

Joining the Oppia research team will enable you to grow your skills, work with an international team of researchers, and positively impact the lives of millions of students.

What we do:

  • Conduct user studies to understand the impact of Oppia’s lessons.
  • Identify and address key issues that students face.
  • Summarize research findings and suggest next steps.
