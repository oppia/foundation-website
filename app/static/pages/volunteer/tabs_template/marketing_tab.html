Oppia's marketing team is responsible for spreading the word about our platform to students, parents, teachers, potential investors and research partners. Raising awareness and building relationships is a key focus of what we do, and helps ensure that our work is useful for students in local communities.

What we do:

  • Raise awareness of Oppia's free lessons among students, teachers and parents.
  • Work closely with local organizations to ensure that our lessons are useful for their students.
  • Find ways to tell Oppia's story, and inspire others to make a positive difference in the world.
