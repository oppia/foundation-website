Effective design is a huge part of making Oppia a powerful tool for learning. We’re on working on a huge variety of projects that requires help from all sort of designers.

Contributing to Oppia is a great opportunity to grow your skills, work with an international team of UX and graphic designers, and positively impact the lives of millions of students.

What we do:

  • Improve the user experience for students and lesson creators
  • Create graphics for lessons
  • Integrate user feedback and new features
