In order to create effective lessons, we need to understand the problems that students are facing. The Oppia team conducts research with students in underserved populations in order to identify the barriers they face, and find ways to remove these barriers.

Joining the Oppia research team will enable you to grow your skills, work with an international team of researchers, and positively impact the lives of millions of students.

What we do:

  • Conduct user studies to gain insights on the effect of Oppia’s lessons.
  • Identify and find ways to address key issues that students face.
  • Write reports that summarize research findings and suggest next steps.
