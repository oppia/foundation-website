The Oppia Foundation

Creating a digital community to help anyone, anywhere learn anything they want in an effective and enjoyable way.

The Oppia Foundation is a community that works to bring students in need closer to effective, enjoyable, and empowering personalized education.

We do this by simulating a private tutor. Oppia provides students the feedback and interaction with a topic that is necessary to attain mastery. Our lessons revolve around engaging and relatable stories that illustrate the real-life importance of a topic. The users learn how skills can be implemented in their everyday lives by working through a variety of everyday scenarios.

Currently, there are lessons available for a range of topics including math, physics, programming, english, accounting, and music technology - and the library is constantly improving and growing! The power of Oppia lies in its open-source community, which is constantly working on existing lessons, creating new ones, or developing the code that drives the platform. This is precisely how we are able to make high-quality education to the undeserved students of the world.

Oppia depends on the community. We encourage you to get involved, or learn more about our mission, team, and impact.
