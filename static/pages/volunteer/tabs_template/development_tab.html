Oppia enables authors to collaborate on creating high-quality lessons at scale. As a developer, you'll be building the technology that makes it all possible. The Oppia codebase is open source, developed by a community of volunteers from all around the world who want to use their skills to make a difference.

Contributing to the Oppia codebase is a great opportunity to grow your skills, work with an international team of developers, and positively impact the lives of millions of students.

What we do:

  • Build features that enable students to learn effectively.
  • Provide infrastructure that can deliver lessons at scale.
  • Apply ML techniques to provide better responses to students' answers.
