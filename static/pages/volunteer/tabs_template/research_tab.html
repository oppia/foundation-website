In order to create effective lessons, we need to understand the problems that students are facing. By researching with students in underserved populations, we are shaping our platform to create a learning environment that addresses their unique challenges and circumstances.

Joining the Oppia research team will enable you to grow your skills, work with an international team of researchers, and positively impact the lives of millions of students.

What we do:

  • Conduct user studies to gain insights on the impact of Oppia’s lessons.
  • Identify and address key issues that students face.
  • Write reports that summarize research findings and suggest next steps.
