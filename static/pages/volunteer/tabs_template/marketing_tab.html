Oppia's marketing team is responsible for spreading the word about our platform to potential investors, research partners, content creators, and of course, students hoping to learn new skills. Raising awareness and building relationships with key stakeholders is an essential part of growing our platform.

Joining the Oppia marketing team will enable you to grow your skills, work with an international team of marketers, and positively impact the lives of millions of students.

What we do:

  • Plan campaigns to raise awareness of Oppia's free lessons among students, teachers and parents.
  • Find ways to share Oppia's story, so that people know that they can make a difference.
  • Ensure that the stories we tell remain true to the organization's mission.
