A new approach to online learning

Oppia is a free and open-source online learning platform for collaboratively creating and sharing interactive lessons.

Our lesson builder allows educators to provide students with targeted, personalized feedback that simulates conversation with an intelligent, virtual tutor.

Visit Oppia.org

Impacting students around the world

Oppia was built to meet the unique needs of the developing world, where educational resources are scarce and methodologies are outdated.

Our storytelling-based approach to learning creates a safe, interactive learning environment for students in underserved communities. And our adaptive lessons require less bandwidth than educational videos, making them more accessible in areas with limited Internet connectivity.

As a global community of volunteers and educators who share a common purpose of improving education for everyone, we continually work to translate Oppia’s lessons into new languages and adapt them for different cultures.

So far, over 1 million learners in more than 140 countries have used the Oppia platform to explore, grow, and learn.
